Jaguar

Jaguars are solitary animals. They are smooth, powerful, and elusive creatures who stalk the Americas and are revered by those who live in their territory. They have a prominent place in mythology and legend due to their alluring stare and hunting prowess. Their beauty, strength, and flexibility have won them admiration from cat lovers all around the world.

Jaguars are mistaken for leorpads because their fur color and markings are similar.
